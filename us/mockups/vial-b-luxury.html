Vial Concept B — Luxury Noir

Dark smoked glass with metallic foil labeling. Inspired by Tom Ford fragrance and Aesop skincare. Premium, aspirational, minimal.

Five Pillars — Showcase Scale
Product Dossier Context
Pillar V — Performance
Ipamorelin
2mg / vial · Lyophilized

Growth hormone secretagogue peptide targeting the pituitary for lean tissue synthesis, recovery acceleration, and age-related decline mitigation. Selective GH release without cortisol or prolactin elevation.

Subcutaneous 30-day cycle Rx required
Design Notes
  • Dark smoked glass body with warm amber gradient
  • Oversized matte black cap, no crimp ring
  • Metallic gold foil label text (no background fill)
  • Pillar color visible at low opacity through dark glass
  • Subtle shadow/glow in pillar color beneath each vial
  • Hover state brightens gold text and lifts vial
