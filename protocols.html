one standard of care

New Clients12 Weeks

Foundation Reset

For new clients beginning a structured training program. This protocol addresses the three systems that fail first: metabolism, recovery, and sleep.

  • Metabolic peptide stack for early body composition changes
  • Recovery compounds to reduce DOMS and training soreness
  • Sleep architecture support through GH secretagogues
  • Dosing calendar synced to training schedule
  • Nutrition protocol and supplement guidance
  • Bi-weekly biomarker check-ins
Active Clients8 Weeks

Performance Edge

For active individuals seeking measurable progression. This protocol targets growth hormone output, cognitive sharpness, and accelerated recovery between sessions.

  • CJC-1295/Ipamorelin GH secretagogue stack
  • Cognitive compounds for focus and training intensity
  • Recovery peptides for inter-session repair
  • Training periodization alignment
  • Body composition tracking at weeks 0, 4, and 8
  • Performance metric benchmarks
Established ClientsOngoing

Longevity Maintenance

For established clients sustaining long-term health. This protocol focuses on cellular repair, neuroprotection, and systemic maintenance that prevents age-related decline.

  • GHK-Cu for collagen and tissue remodeling
  • Epithalon for telomere maintenance cycles
  • Semax/Selank for cognitive preservation
  • Quarterly bloodwork and biomarker panels
  • Annual protocol review and adjustment
  • Priority access to new compound additions
Injury6–8 Weeks

Injury Return

For individuals returning to training after injury or surgery. This protocol accelerates tissue repair and structural rebuilding to reduce time away from the gym.

  • BPC-157 for tissue and ligament repair
  • TB-500 for inflammation and cell migration
  • Joint support compounds as needed
  • Graduated return-to-training schedule
  • Weekly pain and mobility assessments
  • Coordination with physiotherapy where applicable
