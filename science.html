Compound Library

The portfolio

Pillar II — Recovery

BPC-157

Body Protection Compound

Accelerates tendon, ligament, and gut tissue healing with demonstrated angiogenic and anti-inflammatory action. Over 100 preclinical studies support its regenerative profile. One of the most researched compounds in the portfolio.

Pillar I — Longevity

GHK-Cu

Copper Tripeptide

Triggers collagen synthesis, wound repair, and anti-aging gene expression. Research shows activation of over 4,000 genes involved in tissue remodeling, with applications across dermatology, orthopedics, and neuroscience.

Pillar II — Recovery

TB-500

Thymosin Beta-4 Fragment

Promotes cell migration, reduces inflammation, and supports recovery from muscle, tendon, and cardiac tissue damage. The primary mechanism involves upregulation of actin, enabling faster tissue repair and reduced scar formation.

Pillar I — Longevity

Epithalon

Telomerase Activator

Extends telomere length to slow biological aging. The only peptide with direct evidence of lifespan extension in mammalian studies. Works by stimulating telomerase production in somatic cells.

Pillar IV — Cognitive

Semax

Synthetic ACTH Analog

Enhances BDNF expression for cognitive clarity, memory retention, and neuroprotection under physical and psychological stress. Approved as a pharmaceutical in Russia since 2001 for cognitive and vascular conditions.

Pillar V — Performance

CJC-1295 / Ipamorelin

GH Secretagogue Stack

Stimulates natural GH pulsatility for lean mass, fat metabolism, and deep restorative sleep architecture. The combination produces synergistic GH release without appetite stimulation or cortisol increase.

Pillar III — Metabolic

Tesamorelin

GHRH Analog

FDA-approved for visceral adipose tissue reduction. Stimulates the pituitary to produce growth hormone through the natural GHRH pathway, resulting in targeted abdominal fat reduction while preserving lean mass.

Pillar IV — Cognitive

Selank

Tuftsin Analog

Anxiolytic peptide with demonstrated effects on stress resilience, mood regulation, and immune modulation. Operates through GABA-ergic pathways without sedation or dependency risks. Complementary to Semax in cognitive stacks.

Pillar III — Metabolic

AOD-9604

Modified GH Fragment

Modified fragment of human growth hormone targeting fat metabolism without impacting IGF-1 levels or blood sugar. Stimulates lipolysis and inhibits lipogenesis for body composition changes without systemic GH effects.

Pillar III — Metabolic

5-Amino-1MQ

NNMT Inhibitor

Small molecule that inhibits NNMT, an enzyme linked to metabolic dysfunction and obesity. Research demonstrates increased NAD+ levels, enhanced cellular energy metabolism, and potential reversal of diet-induced obesity pathways.

Pillar II — Recovery

KPV

Alpha-MSH Fragment

Anti-inflammatory tripeptide derived from alpha-melanocyte-stimulating hormone. Reduces gut inflammation, supports intestinal barrier function, and modulates immune responses. Particularly effective for inflammatory bowel conditions and systemic inflammation.

Pillar III — Metabolic

Retatrutide

Triple Agonist (GIP/GLP-1/Glucagon)

Triple agonist targeting GIP, GLP-1, and glucagon receptors simultaneously. Clinical trials show weight loss exceeding 24% of body weight. Works through coordinated appetite regulation, glucose metabolism, and increased energy expenditure.
