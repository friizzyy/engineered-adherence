The Gap

Globally, peptide therapeutics are a multi-billion dollar industry. Semaglutide alone exceeded $13B in annual revenue by 2023 and continues to grow. Clinical trials demonstrate 15–24% body weight reduction with these compounds.

Qatar has zero dedicated peptide providers. No structured protocol clinic, no branded adherence platform, no local access to clinically validated compounds. We are building that infrastructure.

The Timing

Post-World Cup, Qatar is investing heavily in health infrastructure and quality of life. The country is building the systems that will define the next decade of wellness in the region.

The platform is positioned to launch while demand is growing and no local competition exists.
