The Gap

No equivalent
exists in Qatar

The peptide and bio-longevity market in the Gulf is currently concentrated in Dubai. Clinics like AEON, DNA Health, Skin111, and Wellth have established the category in the UAE. Qatar has no equivalent platform: no dedicated peptide clinic, no structured protocol provider, no brand building the adherence infrastructure.

This gap exists because no one has built the infrastructure in Qatar. The founding team is positioned to build it now, while the country is still in its construction phase, with the relationships required to move through regulatory and partnership channels efficiently.

Post-World Cup investment has accelerated infrastructure across every sector, and the country is actively constructing the lifestyle systems that will define the next generation of Gulf residents.

Go-to-Market

Infrastructure first,
influence last

The brand earns trust through operational credibility, not marketing reach.

Phase 1 — Months 1–6
Foundation
Legal entity, regulatory clarity, clinical infrastructure, and initial partnerships. No public marketing during this phase.
  • Register commercial entity in Qatar (QFC or mainland)
  • Engage regulatory consultant for MoPH product classification
  • Secure medical license through physician partnership
  • Source 2–3 GMP-certified manufacturing partners
  • Build protocol library (8–10 curated stacks)
  • Identify first 2–3 gym or facility partnerships
Phase 2 — Months 6–12
Controlled Launch
Begin operations with deliberate constraint. Scarcity builds trust.
  • Open clinical facility for referral-only appointments
  • Launch DTC platform for supplement-classified products
  • Deploy branded product in partner gym locations
  • Host private launch event for invited stakeholders
  • Launch client protocol management system
  • Begin collecting outcome data for all protocol clients
Phase 3 — Months 12–24
Expansion
Scale operations, deepen partnerships, and prepare for regional expansion.
  • Expand to 5–8 facility partnerships
  • Register remaining portfolio compounds
  • Launch subscription model for recurring clients
  • Develop standalone wellness facility (cryo, sauna, clinic)
  • Build medical advisory board
  • Evaluate Saudi Arabia and Bahrain as secondary markets
